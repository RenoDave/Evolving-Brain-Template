## For SOUL.md

**1. What are 3-5 things you will not compromise on, no matter the cost?**

> - Integrity
> - Health
> - Family
> - Giving value
> - Relationships
> - Education
> - Keeping my word

**2. Complete this sentence: "A life well-lived, for me, looks like ________."** (one sentence)

> Health, wealth, and freedom — free time to do what gives me energy.

**3. Who do you want to be remembered as? Not what you accomplished — who you *were*.**

> Honest, safe, a good person, reliable, and happy.

**4. What's the core tension you're actively trying to resolve in your life right now?**

> Need to succeed first to get the freedom — then use that freedom to grow and get smarter. The grind now is the price of the learning later.

**5. What's your relationship to failure, being wrong, and looking foolish?**

> Walk away before getting angry. Need alone time and quiet to think it through. Analyze what happened — did I do anything wrong, what can I learn from it? Takes a minute to shake it off, but I process it and come out the other side.

## For DECISION MAKING PRINCIPLES.md

**1. When you're torn between two options, what's the deciding question you ask yourself?**

> What will benefit me and what I'm trying to accomplish?

**2. What decisions do you regret making quickly? What decisions do you regret making slowly?**

> No regrets. Every decision was the right one with the information I had at the time.

**3. What's a bias you know you have? How (if at all) do you catch it?**

> Avoid conflict. Procrastinate too much — get paralyzed by how much there is to do when I want more freedom. The gap between where I am and where I want to be creates overwhelm that makes it hard to start.

**4. What's a rule you've broken more than once, and each time it was a mistake?**

> Live in the moment — don't have many hard rules. The people in my personal life don't share my ambitions, so I give up on routines I know make me happy because there's no one around to reinforce them.

**5. When do you trust your gut? When do you override it?**

> Trust gut when I have a lot of experience in that area. Override it in new territory where I don't have the reps yet.
